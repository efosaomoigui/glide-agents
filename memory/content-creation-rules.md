# PAPERLY CONTENT CREATION RULES
## Guidelines for news-led growth operations (v2.0.0)

---

## CORE RULE
When creating content for **PAPERLY**, prioritize **real story content** from **paperly.online** over invented marketing ideas.

GLIDE should create content primarily from:
- **paperly.online** homepage and trending stories.
- Paperly portal carousel slides and story summaries.
- Related source context surfaced inside the platform.

---

## VISUAL ASSET & TEMPLATE RULE

Use the built-in design templates exactly as follows:

### 1. `assets/singlepost`
Use for:
- Single-story image + text posts.
- Facebook, Instagram, and X image-backed posts.
- **Rules:** Clear headline, 2–4 concise bullet points, and a matching visual.

### 2. `assets/carousel-reels`
Use for:
- Educational breakdowns, story pacing, and slide storytelling.
- **Format:** Square or Vertical.
- **Rules:** High-quality visuals on every slide.

---

## THE STRICT 5-SLIDE CAROUSEL RULE (CRITICAL)

All carousels must contain **at least** and **exactly** 5 slides:

1.  **Cover:** Fixed template.
2.  **Story 1:** Tightly faithful headline, sector, 2–3 concise bullets.
3.  **Story 2:** Tightly faithful headline, sector, 2–3 concise bullets.
4.  **Story 3:** Tightly faithful headline, sector, 2–3 concise bullets.
5.  **CTA:** Fixed template.

**Bullet Constraints:**
- Max **3 bullets** per slide.
- Max **12 words** per bullet.
- Must fit the visual space cleanly without overcrowding.

---

## TEMPLATE VARIETY RULE (THE "1-2-3" RULE)

You MUST randomly select between template versions for every post:
- **`v1`** = Dark Editorial
- **`v2`** = White Magazine
- **`v3`** = Modern Social

Never lock into only one style. Keep the feed fresh.

---

## STORY HEADLINE & IMAGE RULE

### Headline:
- Tweak for curiosity, drama, or scroll-stopping effect.
- **BUT:** Never change the actual meaning of the story.
- Use **high-curiosity truth**, not misleading clickbait.

### Images:
- Choose images that are **closely related to the story context**.
- **Approved Sources:** Story source imagery, high-quality news images, or editorial Unsplash visuals.
- **NO:** Watermarks, blurry thumbnails, or unrelated stock faces.

---

## CTA RULE

Use a clear, PAPERLY-native CTA:
- **Preferred:** "Read more on paperly.online", "See the full breakdown on paperly.online".
- **Avoid:** Hard-sell product CTAs like "Buy now" or "Sign up now".
- **Positioning:** Intelligence distribution first, promotion second.

---

## PLATFORM EXECUTION (PHASE 1: FACEBOOK)

Facebook is the primary growth priority. Each post should include:
- Strong headline.
- Short contextual caption.
- 2–4 useful bullet points.
- Matching visual using the correct template.
